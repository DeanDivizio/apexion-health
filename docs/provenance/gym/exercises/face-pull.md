## `facePull` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (rear delts + mid/lower traps + rhomboids)

- **Code pointer**: `facePull.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Face pulls combine horizontal abduction with external rotation/scapular retraction, often used for posterior deltoid and scapular stabilizers.
- **Sources**
  - Deltoid activation systematic review: `https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1360859222001607`
- **Confidence**: Medium for involved muscles; Low for exact split.

### Attachment / cadence / pause

- **Code pointers**: `facePull.variationEffects.cableAttachment`, `.cadence`, `.pause`
- **Notes**: Evidence is limited and highly attachment/technique dependent; defaults remain neutral/conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low.
