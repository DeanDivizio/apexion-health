## `deadlift` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Sumo (wide) vs conventional → quads/adductors vs posterior chain

- **Code pointer**: `deadlift` → `variationEffects.width`
- **What it supports**: sumo/wider stance tends to increase **quad** contribution; conventional tends to emphasize posterior chain more (hamstring/back differences vary by study).
- **Sources**
  - `https://journals.lww.com/acsm-msse/fulltext/2002/04000/an_electromyographic_analysis_of_sumo_and.19.aspx`
  - `https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/bioengineering-and-biotechnology/articles/10.3389/fbioe.2025.1597209/full`
- **Confidence**: Medium for quads; Low–Medium for lower-back changes (mixed findings).

### Trap bar vs straight bar

- **Code pointer**: `deadlift` → `variationEffects.barType.trapBar`
- **What it supports**: trap bar tends to increase **quad** activation and often reduce hamstring/back demand vs straight bar (study-dependent).
- **Sources**
  - `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/26840440/`
  - `https://journals.lww.com/nsca-jscr/fulltext/2018/03000/electromyographic_comparison_of_barbell_deadlift,.1.aspx`
- **Confidence**: Medium.
