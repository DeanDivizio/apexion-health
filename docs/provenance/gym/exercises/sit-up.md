## `sitUp` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (rectus abdominis + hip flexors)

- **Code pointer**: `sitUp.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Sit-ups generally involve more hip flexor contribution than crunches due to larger hip flexion demands, especially with anchored feet.
- **Sources**
  - Abdominal + hip flexor activation across exercises: `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/9118976/`
  - Upper vs lower rectus EMG during trunk exercises: `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/11319934/`
- **Confidence**: Medium.

### Bench angle / resistance source / cadence / pause

- **Code pointers**: `sitUp.variationEffects.planeAngle`, `.resistanceSource`, `.cadence`, `.pause`
- **Notes**: Angle and anchoring change hip flexor demand; evidence is variable. Defaults remain conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low.
