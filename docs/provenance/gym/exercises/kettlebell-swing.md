## `kettlebellSwing` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (hip hinge power: glutes/hamstrings + trunk)

- **Code pointer**: `kettlebellSwing.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Kettlebell swings are a ballistic hip hinge emphasizing hip extensors (glutes/hamstrings) with trunk musculature contributing for force transfer and control.
- **Sources**
  - Kettlebell swing EMG + kinematics (PMC): `https://ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC4637916/`
  - Trunk muscle activity in kettlebell swing variants: `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/30911671/`
- **Confidence**: Medium for prime movers; Low for exact distribution.

### Stance width / resistance source / cadence

- **Code pointers**: `kettlebellSwing.variationEffects.width`, `.resistanceSource`, `.cadence`
- **Notes**: Evidence for precise muscle redistribution by stance width is limited; deltas are conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low.
