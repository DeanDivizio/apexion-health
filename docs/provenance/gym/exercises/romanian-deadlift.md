## `romanianDeadlift` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (hamstrings + glutes + spinal erectors)

- **Code pointer**: `romanianDeadlift.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Romanian deadlifts emphasize hip hinge with relatively higher hamstring demand than conventional deadlifts, with glute and spinal erector contribution depending on technique and load.
- **Sources**
  - Conventional vs Romanian deadlift EMG + kinetics: `https://ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC6323186/`
  - RDL variant EMG analysis (RDL vs step-RDL vs stiff-leg): `https://www.mdpi.com/1660-4601/19/3/1903`
- **Confidence**: Medium for “hamstrings emphasized”; Low for exact weights.

### Stance width / resistance source / bar type / grip assistance / cadence / pause

- **Code pointers**: `romanianDeadlift.variationEffects.width`, `.resistanceSource`, `.barType`, `.gripAssistance`, `.cadence`, `.pause`
- **Notes**: Evidence for many of these is limited and technique-dependent; deltas are conservative/neutral.
- **Confidence**: Low.
