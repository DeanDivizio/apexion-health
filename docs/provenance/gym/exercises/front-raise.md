## `frontRaise` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (anterior deltoid)

- **Code pointer**: `frontRaise.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Shoulder flexion/front raise primarily targets anterior deltoid, with some lateral deltoid and elbow flexor contribution depending on grip and implement.
- **Sources**
  - Deltoid activation systematic review: `https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1360859222001607`
- **Confidence**: Medium.

### Grip / body position / resistance source / cadence / pause

- **Code pointers**: `frontRaise.variationEffects.grip`, `.bodyPosition`, `.resistanceSource`, `.cadence`, `.pause`
- **Notes**: Many differences are technique-dependent; we keep deltas conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low.
