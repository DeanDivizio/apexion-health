## `lateralRaise` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (lateral deltoid)

- **Code pointer**: `lateralRaise.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Shoulder abduction/lateral raise primarily targets lateral deltoid, with upper trap contribution depending on scapular elevation.
- **Sources**
  - Deltoid activation systematic review: `https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1360859222001607`
- **Confidence**: Medium.

### Body position / range of motion

- **Code pointers**: `lateralRaise.variationEffects.bodyPosition`, `lateralRaise.variationEffects.rangeOfMotion`
- **Notes**: Hip-hinged variations shift the movement plane and can increase posterior deltoid involvement; evidence is mostly biomechanical and exercise-variant specific, so deltas are conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low.

### Resistance source / cadence / pause

- **Code pointers**: `lateralRaise.variationEffects.resistanceSource`, `.cadence`, `.pause`
- **Notes**: Tracked for logging; effects remain neutral due to limited generalizable evidence.
- **Confidence**: Low.
