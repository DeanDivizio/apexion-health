## `backSquat` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Stance width → quads vs glutes/adductors

- **Code pointer**: `backSquat` → `variationEffects.width`
- **What it supports**: wider stances tend to increase glute/adductor contribution; narrower tends to be more quad-dominant (directionally).
- **Sources**
  - `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/19130646/`
  - `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/11404665/`
- **Confidence**: Medium for direction; Low for exact magnitudes.

### Heel elevation → more quad emphasis

- **Code pointer**: `backSquat` → `variationEffects.heelElevation`
- **What it supports**: heel elevation / weightlifting shoes tends to allow more upright squat mechanics with greater knee flexion demand, often interpreted as more quad emphasis.
- **Sources**
  - `https://bmcsportsscimedrehabil.biomedcentral.com/counter/pdf/10.1186/s13102-020-00191-y.pdf`
  - `https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/02640414.2016.1175652`
- **Confidence**: Medium (more about biomechanics than direct “muscle %”).
