## `rearDelt` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (posterior deltoid with scapular retractors)

- **Code pointer**: `rearDelt.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Reverse fly / horizontal abduction emphasizes posterior deltoid, with contribution from scapular retractors (rhomboids / middle trapezius) depending on intent and technique.
- **Sources**
  - Hand position effects during reverse fly / horizontal abduction: `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/23302754/`
  - Deltoid activation systematic review: `https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1360859222001607`
- **Confidence**: Medium.

### Body position / support

- **Code pointers**: `rearDelt.variationEffects.bodyPosition`, `rearDelt.variationEffects.support`
- **Notes**: Support and prone positioning can change stabilization demands and scapular mechanics; direct evidence mapping these changes to precise muscle redistribution is limited. Deltas are conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low.

### Resistance source / cadence / pause

- **Code pointers**: `rearDelt.variationEffects.resistanceSource`, `.cadence`, `.pause`
- **Notes**: Tracked for logging; effects are neutral/conservative due to limited generalizable evidence.
- **Confidence**: Low.
