## `hackSquat` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (quad-dominant squat pattern)

- **Code pointer**: `hackSquat.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Hack squat machines typically provide external trunk support and a fixed path, often reducing posterior-chain and trunk stabilization demands relative to free squats while maintaining high quadriceps demand.
- **Sources**
  - Squat exercise EMG comparison paper (includes machine/variation comparisons): `https://journals.lww.com/acsm-msse/fulltext/1997/04000/emg_analysis_of_lower_extremity_muscle_recruitment.16.aspx`
- **Confidence**: Medium for “quad-dominant, less trunk”; Low for exact weights.

### Stance width / foot height / foot angle

- **Code pointers**: `hackSquat.variationEffects.width`, `.footVerticalPosition`, `.footAngle`
- **Notes**: Foot placement can shift knee vs hip bias; the strongest evidence base is for leg press variants and biomechanics. Deltas are conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low-to-Medium.

### Cadence / pause

- **Code pointers**: `hackSquat.variationEffects.cadence`, `.pause`
- **Notes**: Tracked for logging; effects remain neutral.
- **Confidence**: Low.
