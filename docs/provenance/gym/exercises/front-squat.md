## `frontSquat` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (quads + glutes + trunk)

- **Code pointer**: `frontSquat.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Front squats tend to use a more upright torso and higher knee extensor demand per load, with meaningful trunk bracing demands. EMG comparisons often show **similar quadriceps activation** to back squat at comparable relative intensities, but with different kinematics/load tolerance.
- **Sources**
  - Kinematic + EMG comparison: `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/25630691/`
  - JSC comparison article: `https://journals.lww.com/nsca-jscr/Fulltext/2014/10000/Electromyographic_and_Kinetic_Comparison_of_the.17.aspx`
- **Confidence**: Medium for prime movers; Low for exact distribution weights.

### Stance width / heel elevation / foot angle

- **Code pointers**: `frontSquat.variationEffects.width`, `.heelElevation`, `.footAngle`
- **Notes**: Directional effects (knee vs hip bias) are plausible biomechanically, but exact muscle redistribution magnitudes are uncertain; deltas are conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low-to-Medium.

### Resistance source / cadence / pause

- **Code pointers**: `frontSquat.variationEffects.resistanceSource`, `.cadence`, `.pause`
- **Notes**: Tracked for logging; effects remain neutral/conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low.
