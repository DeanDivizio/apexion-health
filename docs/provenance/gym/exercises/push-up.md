## `pushUp` evidence

Defaults location: `lib/gym/exercises.ts`

### Base targets (chest + triceps + anterior deltoid + core)

- **Code pointer**: `pushUp.baseTargets`
- **Rationale**: Push-ups are a closed-chain horizontal press: pecs and triceps are primary, anterior deltoid assists, and trunk musculature contributes for stabilization.
- **Sources**
  - Hand position EMG study: `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16095413/`
- **Confidence**: Medium (push-up variants and tempo/ROM matter).

### Hand width → triceps vs pecs

- **Code pointer**: `pushUp` → `variationEffects.width`
- **What it supports**: narrow/close hand positions tend to increase **triceps** activation vs wide positions.
- **Sources**
  - `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16095413/`
  - `https://journals.lww.com/nsca-jscr/abstract/2005/08000/comparison_of_muscle_activation_using_various_hand.24.aspx`
- **Confidence**: Medium.

### Body angle (incline/decline-style)

- **Code pointer**: `pushUp` → `variationEffects.planeAngle`
- **Notes**: Elevating feet vs hands shifts load distribution and can change relative shoulder vs chest emphasis; evidence exists but is variable by technique. Deltas are conservative.
- **Confidence**: Low-to-Medium.
